The Journey

11 Days  1,600 km

Curated end to end by Golden Travels — wine country, two safari reserves, the Eastern Cape coast, the Garden Route, and a Cape Town finale.

Day1–2
Stellenbosch Wine Country
Arrive and Into the Vines
50 km from Cape Town — 1 hour
Pick up your vehicle and head straight into wine country. Tastings at Spier or Boschendal, oak-lined streets, and a full second day in Jonkershoek Nature Reserve for scenic hikes and vineyard picnics.
Day3–4
Inverdoorn or Aquila Game Reserve
Into the Wild — Big Five Safari
200 km — 2.5 hours
Afternoon arrival for your first game drive — lions, elephants, rhinos in their natural habitat. Day 4 begins with a sunrise drive, leisurely breakfast at the lodge, and children's educational conservation programs.
Day5–6
Port Elizabeth and Addo Elephant Park
Coastal Drive and Elephant Encounters
500 km — 5.5 hours
A long, spectacular drive along the Eastern Cape coast. Arrive in time for sunset on the beach. Day 6 is all Addo — a self-drive through Africa's most elephant-dense national park, with zebras, buffalo, and black rhino roaming free.
Day7–8
Garden Route — Knysna
The Most Beautiful Drive on Earth
260 km — 3 hours
Drive the Garden Route and arrive in Knysna for the Heads at sunset and fresh seafood by the lagoon. Day 8: Bloukrans Bridge — the world's highest commercial bungee jump. Not mandatory, but absolutely legendary.
Day9–10
Return to Cape Town
Penguins, Table Mountain and the V&A
390 km — 4.5 hours
Stop at Betty's Bay to meet the African penguins at Stony Point — one of the trip's most unexpectedly magical moments. Roll into Cape Town by evening. Final day: Table Mountain, Robben Island, the V&A Waterfront.
Day11
Cape Town — Departure
Farewell South Africa
A final morning in one of the world's most beautiful cities. Return your vehicle, board your flight home — carrying memories that will outlast everything else.
